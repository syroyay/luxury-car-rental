Privacy Policy

No Cookies Collected

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of automated data collection. We do not use analytics tools, advertising trackers, or third-party scripts that monitor your browsing behaviour. Your visit to our website is completely private.

Information You Share With Us

The only personal information we receive is what you choose to share with us directly when you contact us via WhatsApp or phone. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your preferred rental dates and times
  • Your delivery address (hotel, airport, or other location)
  • A copy of your driver's license (required for the rental)
  • Payment details (handled securely at the time of pickup)

How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used solely to process your car rental booking. We use it to:

  • Confirm your reservation and arrange delivery
  • Verify your identity and driving eligibility
  • Communicate with you about your booking

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to any third parties.

Data Storage

Your contact details and booking information are stored only for the duration needed to fulfil your rental. Conversations via WhatsApp and phone are subject to the respective platform's own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to request that we delete any personal information you have shared with us. Simply contact us via WhatsApp or phone and we will remove your data promptly.
